Successful Launch Case Study

We hunt this product for one of our clients last year September 2020 after the hunt we did a patent check of the product and then source product from ALIBABA (almost everyone knows the world biggest trading market) We order our inventory (1500) at last of September after finalizing the supplier, It takes 15 days to manufacture 1500 units between at this time we find Freight forwarder and 3PL in the US for storage because last year amazon set a policy to send just 200 initially on their warehouse that’s why we find 3PL in the US.

Between a gap of 2 months, we make a Perfect listing for our product after deep research of the market and order images from the top photographer.

Our product reached at 1st January 2021 on 3PL then from 3PL we make a shipment plan to amazon warehouse, finally, our product reached on 12th January and live on amazon now time begins to rank a product as soon as possible before ending honeymoon period (amazon gives 30 days to new sellers to rank quickly on market).

Finally with hard work of our team from the last 2 months and the perfect strategy makes it easy to rank on top 5 within 2 weeks of launch and Amazon choice badge on all main keywords within a month which makes it easier to gain sales on market.

One more thing you need just patience when you are launching your private label, because at the first time when you are entering a market where already sellers are selling and you’re distracting their sales and share market with them so it takes time to stable in the market if you are going to launch a new brand.

At this time our client was extremely happy and we are handling his account and soon we will launch another product for him in UK market.
